English indie rock band formed in 1988 in London and disbanded in 1997. Band name stylized as The SUNDAYS on all releases.

Members:
Harriet Wheeler (vocals)
David Gavurin (guitar)
Paul Brindley (bass)
Patrick Hannan (drums)
