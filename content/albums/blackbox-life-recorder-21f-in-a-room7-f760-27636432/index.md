Blackbox Life Recorder 21f / In a Room7 F760 is an EP by British electronic music artist Richard D. James, released under the pseudonym Aphex Twin on 28 July 2023 on Warp. It was preceded by the release of the first track and single "Blackbox Life Recorder 21f" on 21 June 2023. The EP contains four tracks, including an alternate "Parallax mix" of "Blackbox Life Recorder 21f" as the final track.


    
    


## Tracklisting
1. Blackbox Life Recorder 21f
2. Zin2 Test5
3. In A Room7 F760
4. Blackbox Life Recorder 22 (Parallax Mix)
