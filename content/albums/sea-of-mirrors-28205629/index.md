Sea of Mirrors is the upcoming eleventh studio album by English rock band the Coral. It is set to be released on 8 September 2023 through Run On and Modern Sky. The album was produced by Sean O'Hagan and features guest appearances by former member Bill Ryder-Jones, Cilian Murphy, John Simm and Love guitarist Johnny Echols.
