German 2016 repress on gold vinyl, sourced from the [2014 Optimal Media GmbH stampers](https://www.discogs.com/Kyuss-Welcome-To-Sky-Valley/release/6681326).  This release is missing the Hidden Track 'Lick Doo' that is present after 'Whitewater' on many Issues of this album .  The writing credits of "Josh Homme" are erroneously credited to "John Homme" on the vinyl label. They are correctly printed on the back cover though.
