Diving Deep Live is the 6th live album by American singer-songwriter Tori Amos. It was released on December 6, 2024, through Decca Records. The album was recorded on Amos' Ocean to Ocean Tour throughout 2022 and 2023 backed by bassist and "musical director" Jon Evans and drummer Ash Soan. The standard LP will contain twelve tracks, while CD and digital releases, under the name Diving Deeper, feature four bonus tracks. "Cornflake Girl" was released as the album's single on November 1, 2024.
