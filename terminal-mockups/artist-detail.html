CHINA CRISIS

Electronic Pop New Wave Liverpool

BIOGRAPHY

Arty synth-rock group formed in Liverpool, UK in 1979 and comprising Gary Daly (vocals), Eddie Lundon (guitar), Brian McNeill (keyboards), Gazza Johnson (bass) and Kevin Wilkinson (drums).

The band emerged from the post-punk scene in Liverpool, developing a distinctive sound that combined melodic pop sensibilities with sophisticated electronic arrangements. Their music often featured prominent use of synthesizers, drum machines, and Gary Daly's distinctive vocals.

China Crisis achieved commercial success in the early 1980s with hits like "Christian" and "Wishful Thinking," and their music became synonymous with the new romantic movement of the era.
